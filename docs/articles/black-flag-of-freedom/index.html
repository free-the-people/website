The Black Flag of Freedom

 

When the "Black Flag of Freedom" flies, it sends a warning to the United States. This simple flag makes a simple statement:

I do not agree with the actions the United States of America is taking right now!

 

For the people:
It is a flag of protest. A flag of dissent. A flag of outrage. And it is a call to action for all patriots.

For those in authority:
It is a flag of warning. A flag of imminent danger. It says, "you've crossed the line, and your people are angry."

Significantly, this flag says:

"Even though I may disagree with the actions the United States is engaged in, I still have respect for the principles upon which it was founded."

The principcles the United States was founded on are outlined in the Charters of Freedom. Chief among these principles are the rule of constitutional law and the fundamental rights of all people.

The Black Flag of Freedom's Design

We use the American flag as our symbol to reflect our pride in the founding principles of the United States of America, but intentionally alter it to represent our protest of the current state of the Union.

We chose to replace the white stripes with black stripes to represent our sense of disgrace and outrage over whichever action(s) the United States is taking that we are protesting when we raise this flag. This country has a long history of engaging in oppression, aggression, and imperialism, both at home and abroad. Such actions are in direct violation of the foundational principles of this country and must be redressed.

The color white in the American flag is broadly accepted as symbolizing purity and innocence. We have kept the color white in the 50 stars to ensure this symbolism is maintained. But we also chose to replace the white stripes with black to indicate those very values of purity and innocence are currently being tarnished.

When to Fly the Black Flag of Freedom

This flag is appropriate to fly any time you wish to signal your disagreement with the actions taken in the name of the United States. This flag is not specific to a single cause or a single movement. It is a universal way to denounce the United States' actions while still respecting the dignity and rights of the people living in or affiliated with the United States.

Today, free-the-people.us flies this flag as an act of legal protest, protected by our constitutional right to free speech, to denounce the authoritarian regime that currently holds unprecidented, unconstitutional, and unlawful political power.

Around the world, the Black Flag of Freedom can and should be flown to protest American imperialism against, oppression of, or aggression towards any nation, group, or individual in violation of the founding principles of the United States.

The Black Flag of Freedom is a flag of general outrage. Ideally, it will be flown alongside other flags that represent more targeted protests. For example, when protesting the totalitarian takeover of the United States, it should be flown alongside flags from Palestine, flags from our Central and South American neighbors, the rainbow LGBTQ+ flag, the trans pride flag, the upside-down American flag, the standard American flag, and many others because this regime is trampling on the rights of all of these groups.
