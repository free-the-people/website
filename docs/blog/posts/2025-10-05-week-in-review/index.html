The bad...

Government shuts down without budget resolution [Link]

Image by Gerd Altmann from Pixabay.

The U.S. government has officially shut down as Congress fails to come to an agreement on a funding bill. Republicans expect Democrats to accept the funding bill they created without any Democratic feedback, and Democrats expect Republicans to make concessions and extend subsidies for Affordable Care Act health insurance policies for American citizens.

While the government is shut down, government employees will not receive their paychecks, except for Members of Congress who continue to be paid. Unpaid employees fall into two categories: Those that are temporarily ordered not to report to work, and those who are considered essential personnel and are required to continue working without pay. But Donny 2-Dolls is weighing the options for adding a third category: Just fire the furloughed employees. The Trump regime has been rashly firing federal employees since taking control of the country, and now they are using this shutdown to inflict even more pain on the American people.

While Democrats disagree with most of the provisions of the Republican spending bill, the main concession they are asking for is to extend subsidies for health insurance policies that are set to expire at the end of the year. If these subsidies expire, millions of Americans will see their health care policies double or even triple in price next year.

The ugly...

ICE delivers a message to America: "F#@k them kids" [Link]

In one of many nightmarish scenes just this week, hundreds of armed ICE thugs wearing military-style costumes descended on an apartment complex in the middle of the night along with riot trucks and helicopters. They deployed smoke grenades, broke through fences and stormed the building, dragging residents out of their homes at gunpoint to stand outside in little to no clothing for hours or loading them like cattle into unmarked rental vans and hauling off to undisclosed locations.

Some of those rounded up may have been undocumented, but many of them were certainly legal U.S. residents. In all, 37 people were arrested. Children were tied together with zip-ties while agents laughed and one was heard to say, "Fuck them kids."
