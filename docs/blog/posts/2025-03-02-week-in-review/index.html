Connect to your representative

House Republicans are pushing a bill, called the SAVE Act that intends to disenfranchise millions of eligible American voters.

Under the guise of election security, the SAVE Act would require proof of citizenship to be presented to register to vote. When that's all you hear, it sounds very reasonable. But there are problems with this:

  • Not everyone can access these documents (They are frequently lost or destroyed)
  • They must be presented in person, eliminating online and mail-in voter registration
  • It would prevent most married women from being able to register to vote if they changed their names when they married
  • It makes it easier to intimidate and add barriers to naturalized citizens
  • It just makes it harder in general, so fewer people will register to vote

Indivisible has an online form you can fill out to get connected directly to your representative so you can tell them to vote NO on this dangerous bill.
