Assault with a sandwich? Nah. [Link]

Sean Dunn throws a sandwich at a CBP officer in Washington D.C. on August 10, 2025.

On August 10, Sean Dunn was captured on video in a verbal confrontation with a CBP officer in Washington D.C. A frustrated Dunn tossed his Subway sandwich at the officer and was subsequently chased down and detained. He was initially charged with felony assault, but a grand jury refused to indict him on such a ridiculous charge, so the charges were dropped to misdemeanor assault.

Dunn was found not guilty of the equally ridiculous misdemeanor charge in his trial. The defense argued that a sandwich simply cannot be wielded with sufficient force to pose a credible threat to a man wearing- a bullet proof vest.

Dunn has become something of a symbol of the anti-Trump regime, with murals, signs, and banners depicting him throwing a sandwich appearing all around the country.

What is Democratic Socialism? [Link]

The Fist and Rose: A common symbol of democratic socialism. Image by RoofOfAllLight from Wikimedia Commons. Licensed under the Creative Commons CC0 1.0 Universal Public Domain Dedication license.

The next mayor of New York City is a self-proclaimed Democratic Socialist. So is Senator Bernie Sanders. And Representative Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez. But what does it mean to be a democratic socialist?

The simple truth is that, like most political ideologies, there is no single, absolute, definitive definition of the term. There's lots of room for interpretation and disagreement even among people who identify with the label. Broadly speaking, democratic socialists believe that certain sectors of the economy (such as healthcare, housing, and transportation) cannot provide the public benefit necessary if they are run solely for profit.

The primary purpose of democratic socialism is to curb capitalism's excesses through progressive reforms that target human rights and dignity over profitability.
